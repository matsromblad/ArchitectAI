How it works

Five milestones from brief to BIM — fully automated, quality-gated at every step.

📋 M1
Brief
Describe the building in plain language. Upload a site plan.
📐 M2
Layout
The Architect Agent places rooms and designs circulation.
⚙️ M3
Systems
Structural and MEP agents coordinate technical systems.
🏛️ M4
Model
IFC Builder generates a complete BIM model.
✅ M5
Export
Download your validated IFC4 file.

Why AI Nightingale

Built for real projects

Not just a demo — a production-grade pipeline designed for architects and AEC firms.

🌍
Global by default
One system for Sweden, UAE, UK. The Compliance Agent adapts to any jurisdiction automatically — no manual configuration needed.
🔄
Iterative by design
QA gates and feedback loops ensure quality at every step. Agents revise their outputs until they meet the standard — or escalate to the PM.
📂
Open format
IFC4 output opens in Revit, ArchiCAD, Vectorworks, Solibri — no lock-in. Your model belongs to you, in an industry-standard format.
